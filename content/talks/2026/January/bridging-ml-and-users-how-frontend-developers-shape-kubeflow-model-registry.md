## Abstract

In this session, we'll explore how your Typerscript, React, and UI/UX skills can make a real impact in the MLOps world through Kubeflow Model Registry. You'll discover:

* Why frontend matters in ML: How good UI/UX bridges the gap between complex ML systems and their users
* Kubeflow Model Registry architecture: Understanding the frontend stack (React, TypeScript, Material-UI) and how it connects to backend APIs
* Real contribution opportunities:
  * Improving model comparison visualizations
  * Building intuitive model versioning interfaces
  * Enhancing metadata display and search functionality
* Creating better deployment workflows
* Live walkthrough: Navigating the codebase, setting up dev environment, and making your first UI contribution
* Open source tips: Finding good first issues, working with the Kubeflow community, and getting your PRs merged
